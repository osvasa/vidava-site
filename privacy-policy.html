Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

What VIDAVA Does NOT Access

We do not collect, store, or have access to any sensitive financial data. At no point does VIDAVA receive, transmit, or retain card numbers, CVV/CVC security codes, expiration dates, billing addresses, or cardholder names as they appear on your card.

We do not establish connections to your bank accounts, financial institutions, or payment processors. No transaction data, account balances, or fund-transfer capabilities ever pass through our systems.

The only card-related information we store is what you voluntarily enter: the card's common name (e.g. "Chase Sapphire Preferred"), the issuing bank, and rewards details. This limited data is used solely to generate personalized recommendations — the actual payment at checkout is handled entirely by the merchant and your bank.

What We Collect

VIDAVA collects only the information needed to provide personalized card recommendations:

  • Your email address (for account authentication)
  • Credit card details you provide (card name, bank, rewards type, APR, payment due date, and credit limit)
  • Checkout page data (store name, purchase category, and order total) — analyzed locally in your browser to generate recommendations

How We Use Your Data

Your information is used for one purpose: to recommend the best card to use at checkout. Specifically:

  • Card details are stored securely in the cloud so they sync across your devices
  • Checkout page data is sent to our AI service to determine which of your cards earns the most rewards for that purchase
  • We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond delivering recommendations

We Do Not Sell Your Data

VIDAVA does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your card details and browsing activity are never monetized.

Data Storage and Security

Your account and card data are stored securely via Supabase, a cloud database platform with encryption at rest and in transit. AI recommendations are processed through Anthropic's Claude API — no personal data is stored by Anthropic or used for model training.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • You can view, edit, or delete any card from the extension at any time
  • You can delete your account entirely by contacting us at hello@vidava.ai — we will remove all associated data from our systems
  • You can uninstall the extension at any time, which stops all data collection immediately

Cookies and Tracking

VIDAVA does not use cookies, analytics trackers, or third-party tracking scripts. The extension only activates on checkout pages to provide card recommendations.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised date. Continued use of VIDAVA after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about your privacy? Reach us at hello@vidava.ai.
